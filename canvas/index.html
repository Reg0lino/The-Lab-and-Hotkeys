Additional Info & Lyrical Pacing Guidelines Additional Info

The Zoom Method (Micro to Macro) Start with an ultra-close physical detail (drywall flakes), then expand to a wide shot (neon horizon).
The W3 Lyric Formula Define Who is speaking, To Whom they are speaking, and Why they must speak right now to anchor your verse.
Gibberish Automatic Writing Track open-voiced vowels over looped progressions, preserve the dynamic vocal "feel", then map words to match.
Decoupled Rhythm Vamping Tap out a physical vocal rhythm (no pitches) over a loop first, then assign scale notes to those exact hit points.

These core methodologies prevent melodic and structural writer's block during recording sessions.
